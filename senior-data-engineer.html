Senior Data Engineer (Remote)

Trevco Inc.

About the Role

Trevco Inc. is seeking an experienced Senior Data Engineer to lead our data integration and ETL processes in a fully remote capacity. You’ll build pipelines that collect, transform, and centralize data from platforms like Amazon Seller Central, NetSuite, and PostgreSQL into a secure warehouse, enabling reporting in Power BI. This role collaborates across teams (BI, Finance, Ops, Engineering) to drive data-informed decisions company-wide.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and maintain robust ETL/ELT pipelines across Amazon, NetSuite, PostgreSQL, and more
  • Automate workflows using AWS Glue, Airflow, or similar orchestration tools
  • Model scalable data warehouse schemas optimized for Power BI
  • Ensure data quality through validation, alerting, and anomaly detection
  • Develop integrations with external APIs (Amazon SP-API, NetSuite, etc.)
  • Collaborate with BI to prepare clean datasets and performance-optimized views
  • Implement data backup and governance policies
  • Mentor team members and recommend improvements to the data stack
  • Partner with stakeholders to refine data requirements and troubleshoot issues

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • 5+ years of data engineering or BI engineering experience
  • Hands-on experience with AWS Glue and PySpark/Python
  • Proficiency in SQL and Python for data workflows
  • Experience with PostgreSQL, Redshift, or Snowflake
  • Knowledge of NetSuite APIs and Amazon Seller Central data
  • Strong understanding of data modeling and warehouse design
  • Experience structuring data for Power BI and writing DAX queries
  • Familiarity with orchestration tools (Airflow, Step Functions, etc.)
  • Excellent remote communication and problem-solving skills

Nice to Haves

  • Apache Airflow or DAG development experience
  • Knowledge of AWS Redshift, Athena, Lambda, DMS
  • Experience with Spark, Hadoop, or Databricks
  • CI/CD for data jobs and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
  • Deep ERP knowledge (NetSuite, SAP, Oracle)
  • Retail or e-commerce data experience
  • AWS certifications or relevant credentials
  • Leadership or mentoring experience
