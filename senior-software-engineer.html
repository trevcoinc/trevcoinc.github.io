Senior Software Engineer - Internal Applications & Integrations (Remote)

Trevco Inc.

About the Role

Trevco Inc. is seeking a talented Senior Software Engineer to lead the development of internal tools and systems integrations that power our day-to-day operations. As we modernize and scale our internal platforms, you'll build software that has a direct impact on our fulfillment, operations, and scalability—while working in a flexible, remote-first environment with a strong engineering culture.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead development of internal web applications using Laravel and modern PHP
  • Architect scalable, maintainable, and testable systems with a strong focus on developer experience
  • Manage projects from design through deployment, ensuring code quality and performance
  • Build and maintain integrations between internal systems and third-party services (e.g., NetSuite, Amazon SP-API)
  • Monitor and optimize data pipelines, queues, and background processes using tools like SQS, Laravel Horizon, and AWS Lambda
  • Mentor other developers and promote software engineering best practices
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including operations, data, and leadership
  • Improve internal processes and developer workflows through automation, CI/CD, and documentation

Requirements

  • 5+ years of professional experience in software development
  • Strong hands-on experience with Laravel and the broader PHP ecosystem
  • Solid understanding of cloud-native development using AWS (e.g., S3, RDS, SQS, Lambda)
  • Comfortable working with containerization (Docker), Git, and CI/CD pipelines
  • Proven ability to lead engineering initiatives and deliver business value through software
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Nice to Haves

  • Experience with Laravel tools like Forge, Horizon, Livewire, and Filament
  • Familiarity with Ruby on Rails, Go, or Python
  • Understanding of eCommerce, ERP, or fulfillment systems (e.g., NetSuite)
  • Exposure to AI or automation technologies, or interest in applying them to internal tools

About Trevco

Trevco Inc. is a vertically-integrated, global print-on-demand licensee powering licensed merchandise across 1,200+ brands. From design to distribution, we provide turnkey e-commerce solutions with over 300 product types. Our core values – Think Differently, Execute with Urgency, Aspire for More, and Make Work a Great Place – define our team and remote-first culture. Join us to innovate and grow at the intersection of technology and retail.

  • Optional medical, dental, vision, and FSA
  • 401(k) plan
  • Generous PTO and paid holidays
